Zebrafish projector:
1.Run ProjectionGUI.
2.Set the Position Parameter and Click 'Project' button. A image would be project into your 2nd monitor.
3.Set the pattern parameter and click 'Generate Movie'.Wait until the movie is generated, you will see a movie name in the Movie Pool.
4.Choose one movie in the movie pool, click 'Preview'.

The Play button is not finished yet, movie can only be played by 'Preview'.
